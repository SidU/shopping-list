# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 1, 2026*

## What We Collect

**Account Information**
- Email address (via Google Sign-In)
- Google profile name and photo

**Shopping Data**
- Stores you create
- Shopping list items and sections
- Learned item-to-section preferences

**Usage Data**
- API access logs (for rate limiting)

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide and sync your shopping lists across devices
- To share lists with people you invite
- To improve the app experience

## Data Storage

Your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) servers in the United States.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data:
- With people you explicitly share stores with
- With service providers (Firebase, Vercel) to operate the app
- If required by law

## API Access

If you generate an API key, third-party services you authorize can access your shopping data.

## Data Deletion

Delete your account and all data in Settings. You can also delete individual stores at any time.
